Computing and Information Technology at KSU

Kennesaw State University's College of Computing and Software Engineering is one of the best in the country, with each of its degree programs being recognized nationally. We offer both undergraduate and graduate degrees in Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering, Computer Game Design and Development, and Data Science and Analytics. 

Welcome our New Chairs!

  • Information Technology

    Information Technology Chair, Dr. Shaoen Wu has a Ph.D. from Auburn University. He is currently a State Farm Endowed professor of Cybersecurity at the school of Information Technology at Illinois State University. Dr. Wu is a Founding Director, Center for Cybersecurity Research and Education.

    • Software Engineering and Game Development

      Software Engineering and Game Development Chair, Dr. George Markowsky received his Ph.D. in Mathematics from Harvard University. He was a Chair and Professor of Computer Science at Missouri University of Science & Technology. Before joining Missouri S&T, he was the founding Chair of the Computer Science Department at the University of Maine, where he served as Chair for many years.

      • Computer Science Chair

        Computer Science Chair, Dr. Yong Pei received his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY and currently holds the Endowed Chair of LexisNexis Ohio Eminent Scholar for Advanced Data Management and Analysis at Wright State University. He served as the Graduate Program Director of Ph.D. in Computer Science and in the Engineering program.
